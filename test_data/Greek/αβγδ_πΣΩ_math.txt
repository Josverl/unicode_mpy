# Greek Math Symbols - αβγδ πΣΩ

This file tests Greek letters used in mathematics.

## Type: Greek Letters (Mathematical Use)
Lowercase: α β γ δ ε ζ η θ ι κ λ μ ν ξ ο π ρ σ τ υ φ χ ψ ω
Uppercase: Α Β Γ Δ Ε Ζ Η Θ Ι Κ Λ Μ Ν Ξ Ο Π Ρ Σ Τ Υ Φ Χ Ψ Ω

## Common in Math:
- π (pi) - circle constant
- Σ (sigma) - summation
- Ω (omega) - ohms/big-O notation
- θ (theta) - angles
- λ (lambda) - wavelength
