# En Dash and Em Dash Test - A–B A—B

This file tests various dashes and hyphens.

## Type: Dashes and Hyphens
- - Hyphen-Minus (U+002D)
- ‐ Hyphen (U+2010)
- – En Dash (U+2013)
- — Em Dash (U+2014)
- ― Horizontal Bar (U+2015)
- − Minus Sign (U+2212)

## Sample:
- 2020–2025 (range)
- New York—London (break)
- Self-esteem (compound)
